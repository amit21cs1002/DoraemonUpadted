This is doraemon

Doraemon" or simply Doraemon (ドラえもん Doraemon) is the titular deuteragonist of all Doraemon media (including, but not limited to the original manga, anime, video games, and movies). From this history, Doraemon is sent back in time to take care of Nobita on the behalf of the latter's great-great-grandson, Sewashi so he can get a better future and improve Nobita's own future as well.

Characters

Nobita

Nobi (野比 のび太のび のびた Nobi Nobita), known simply as Noby (a possible diminutive of his Japanese name) in the American and UK versions, is the main protagonist of the Doraemon franchise. He was a failure of a person until Doraemon came from the 22nd century to aid him so he could have a better future in life. Nobita is voiced by Yoshiko Ōta in the 1973 series, Noriko Ohara from 1979-March 2005, and Megumi Ōhara from April 2005 onwards.

Gian

Takeshi Gouda (剛田 武ごうだ たけし Gōda Takeshi), more commonly known by his nickname Jaian or Gian, is one of the main character of the Doraemon franchise. Gian sometimes serves as the primary antagonist in some Manga chapters and anime episodes when he is bad or evil in the franchise.

Shizuka

Shizuka Minamoto (源 静香みなもと しずか Minamoto Shizuka), also known by her nickname Sue in the American and UK versions, is the tritagonist of the Doraemon franchise, being the only main female character. In the future after marrying Nobita, she is also known as Shizuka Nobi or Mrs. Nobi.

Suneo

Suneo Honekawa is one of the main protagonists of the Doraemon franchise. He is a rich and spoiled child who was born on February 29th. He likes to brag about the good things he have while other don't, and is often seen with Gian, being forced to bully Nobita with him, though deep down he fears and despises him as much as everyone else and actually feels sorry for Nobita.
